HOW TO REDUCE FOOD WASTE

We throw away 7.2 million tons of food every year, and more than half of it’s perfectly edible. Meanwhile, 1 in 7 people across the world don't have enough to eat. Everyday supermarkets throw away lots of food which expiration dates ended. Meanwhile, that food is still edible. Food industry is one of the most dangerous factory for our environment and economy.
